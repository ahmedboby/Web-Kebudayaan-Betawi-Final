ABOUT THIS FILM

"Finn, a gloomy 22-year-old blog writer who lives a lonely, monotone life and spends most of his time in front of his laptop screen, crosses paths with Chloe, a lively and care-free 21-year-old artist who appreciates and sees the beauty in every little thing.

The mere encounter between the 2 strangers blossoms into something special, and they soon become lovers. Through Chloe, Finn learns to enjoy life and see the world in a brighter prespective.

However, fate intervenes — Chloe falls ill, and nothing in the world could have prepared Finn for the next part — the part where she dies and he is left alone with only the reminiscence of her."
